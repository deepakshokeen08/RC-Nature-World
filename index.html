RC Nature World is located in Mangalpur village, Jind, Haryana. The easiest way is to reach Narwana Junction by train and then take a local taxi or auto to the farm. We are only 10 km from Narwana station and the roads are smooth and beautiful so reaching here feels simple from the start.

Your stay includes food, accommodation and a real village experience. You get space to slow down, breathe deeper and feel connected again. You can enjoy village culture, peaceful mornings, simple living, yoga, meditation and quiet time. Many guests also love listening to stories and learning about Ayurveda, Bhagavad Gita and traditional wisdom.

Yes we provide meals. Food is simple, sattvic and farm-style made with love. We cook traditional Haryana style meals using seasonal ingredients, organic produce when available and local sourcing where needed. Guests often tell us the food feels warm, comforting and unforgettable.

Yes it is very safe. Many of our guests are solo travelers and international guests. We host you like family and we guide you throughout your stay so you feel comfortable from the first day.

Timings are flexible because we understand trains and buses do not always match fixed schedules. We are available 24/7 for support. Still we recommend arriving in daylight and leaving in daylight for the smoothest travel experience.

We mainly focus on overnight stays because this is not a quick sightseeing spot. It is an experience that becomes meaningful through evenings, conversations, slow mornings and calm nights.

You can do many things depending on your mood and energy. You can explore farming, soil work, birdwatching and village walks. You can spend time with cows, feed them and enjoy the calm they bring. You can also join cooking, yoga, meditation, cultural exchange and nearby heritage visits.

Yes it is amazing for families and kids. Children naturally love the open space, the farm environment and the animals. Parents love how peaceful and safe the atmosphere feels.

Phone networks work well here. WiFi is available but it is limited on purpose so you can detox, rest and reconnect. If you need WiFi for work or urgent tasks we will help you with it.
